Advantages

  • Fully Managed: Minimal infrastructure overhead, focus on ML code.

  • Elastic Scalability: Scales up/down automatically with traffic.

  • Pay-per-Use: No upfront hardware investment required.

Challenges

  • Cost at Scale: Can become expensive with 24/7 high-volume inference.

  • Latency: Network round-trip adds 100ms+, critical for real-time.

  • Data Privacy: Data must leave premises to reach cloud endpoints.
