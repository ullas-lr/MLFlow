Part 2 Edge AI

What is Edge AI?

Definition & Core Concepts

Definition

Edge AI is the deployment of AI workflows on local devices, processing data where it is created, rather than sending it to a centralized cloud.

Common Edge Devices

Hospital Servers
Medical Devices
Mobile/Tablets
IoT Gateways

Key Characteristics

Local Processing

Computations happen directly on the device hardware.

Ultra-Low Latency

Real-time inference (< 100ms) without network round-trips.

Privacy-First Design

Sensitive data (PHI) never needs to leave the premises.

Works Offline

Reliable operation even without internet connectivity.

Reduced Bandwidth

Only insights are transmitted, not raw high-res data.
