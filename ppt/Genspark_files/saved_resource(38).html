Summary Session Recap

Key Takeaways

Learning Outcomes

Cloud Platforms

AWS, Azure, and GCP provide robust managed services for scalable ML training and deployment.

Edge AI Benefits

Critical for healthcare due to privacy (local data), low latency (<10ms), and offline reliability.

Hybrid Architecture

The "Best of Both Worlds" approach: Inference at the edge for speed, training in the cloud for scale.

Automated Validation

Systematic testing of Quality, Safety, and Relevance ensures models are production-ready.

Traceability with MLflow

Full audit trails of experiments, parameters, and metrics are essential for compliance and debugging.

Drift Detection

Continuous statistical monitoring prevents silent failures from data shifts or performance degradation.

Deployment Flexibility

The same MLOps pipeline and validation logic works seamlessly across Edge and Cloud environments.

HIPAA Compliance

Achievable in both architectures via local processing (Edge) or BAA/Encryption (Cloud).
